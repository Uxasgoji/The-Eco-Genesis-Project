Rules and Regulations

When visiting our islands it important that you obey the rules present as to refrain from causing any incidents, injuries, or disturbing the ecosystems under our care.

  • When on trails or camping grounds, stay within designated trails and sites.
  • Do not disobey intructors, tour guides, or other staff.
  • Do not leave anything or litter when outside, it can affect the plants and animals present.
  • Do not tamper with any equipment or technology, you can be fined for breaking our property.
  • Never interact with or make physical contact with the organisms present unless permitted by your istructor or tour guide.
